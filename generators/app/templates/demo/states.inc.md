# States

You can mark a section, with a comment containing `#state <state>`,
as in one of the following states:

* `todo` <!-- #state todo -->
* `selected` <!-- #state selected -->
* `open` <!-- #state open -->
* `in-progress` <!-- #state in-progress -->
* `closed` <!-- #state closed -->
* `invalid` <!-- #state invalid -->
* `failed` <!-- #state failed -->
* `rejected` <!-- #state rejected -->
